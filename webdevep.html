WEB DEVELOPMENT


WHAT IS WEBDEVELOPMENT ?

Web development, also known as website development, refers to the tasks associated with creating, building, and maintaining websites and web applications that run online on a browser. It may, however, also include web design, web programming, and database management.

WHAT ARE THE THINGS TO BE LEARNED FOR APPDEVELOPMENT ?

Knowledge of programming languages: Get ready to become an expert in languages like HTML,CSS,JAVASCRIPT,etc. Logical thinking: App Developers often have to figure out how to make different functions work across a variety of devices and operating systems. If you love solving problems, you'll excel in this career.
